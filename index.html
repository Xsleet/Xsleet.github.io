I am a Ph.D. candidate in Communication and Information Systems at Beihang University (BUAA), advised by Academician Shi Chuang. Research

Space-based Autonomous Elastic Timescale Construction and Maintenance for LEO Constellations
低轨星座天基自主弹性时间基准建立与维持理论方法研究

Sep 2024 – Present
Background: Timescale is the core foundation for synchronizing satellites in a constellation. LEO mega-constellations feature ultra-large scale and rapid dynamics, while low-cost miniaturized platforms constrain onboard clock performance, posing critical challenges to conventional ground-dependent timescale construction. System vulnerability to space-environment disturbances and adversarial interference further highlights the need for autonomous, elastic solutions.

Contributions: In-depth analysis of low-cost LEO satellite clock error characteristics under complex near-Earth space environments; precise LEO clock error modeling and prediction via Fourier neural networks; proposal of a space-based autonomous timescale architecture based on heterogeneous low-SWaP-C clock ensembles and inter-satellite links (ISLs), achieving robust timescale realization independent of GNSS and ground infrastructure.

High-Precision GNSS Positioning, Navigation, and Timing (PNT) Integrity Monitoring
北斗高精度定位导航授时可信监测理论方法研究

Sep 2020 – Present
Background: GNSS provides low-cost, high-precision PNT services with broad societal impact. However, inherent GNSS vulnerabilities—including signal errors, multipath, and interference—limit the reliability of positioning and timing services. Systematic integrity monitoring is essential to ensure safe and trustworthy GNSS applications.

Contributions: On the service side: quality monitoring of satellite orbit/clock corrections and ionospheric corrections in wide-area real-time precise positioning, with quality flags broadcast to users. On the user side: proposal of a Timing Advanced RAIM (T-ARAIM) algorithm extending classical ARAIM to high-precision timing integrity monitoring, spanning risk budget allocation, timing solution estimation, and timing protection level computation.
